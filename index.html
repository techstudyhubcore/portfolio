About Me

I'm a B.Pharm graduate (RGUHS, 2025, CGPA 7.2/10) from Koppal, Karnataka — working at the intersection of pharmaceutical knowledge, AI tools, and vernacular health education. 01 · Problem

The Real Gap

Pharmacy and nursing students in Tier 2/3 regions face a structural problem — not a motivation problem:

  • →Scattered, unstructured exam content — no single reliable source
  • →Pharmacology concepts taught only in English — inaccessible to vernacular learners
  • →No PYQ-driven learning system connecting study → revision → exam
  • →Zero digital guidance for first-generation pharmacy students in Koppal-tier districts
👥

  • →Karnataka-region pharmacy college students

Secondary Users

  • →Semester and competitive exam aspirants
  • →Students needing digital academic guidance
